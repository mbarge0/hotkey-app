### Risk: "This is too technical for most creators"
**Mitigation:** 
- Start with technical audience (indie makers)

- Offer premium setup service

### Risk: "AI content gets no engagement"
**Mitigation:**
- Profile-driven (not generic AI)
- Platform-specific optimization
- Prove it works with our own engagement data
- Show voice consistency

**Mitigation:**

- Gauge interest before building paid product

### Risk: "OpenAI/Anthropic builds this into their products"

- We have scheduling intelligence
- First-mover advantage in indie maker space
- Can pivot to vertical-specific solutions
