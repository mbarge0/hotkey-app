Questions?

How does the Chrome extension work?

Install it from the Chrome Web Store. When you're in ChatGPT or Claude, click the HotKey icon. It captures your recent conversation context, you can add a screenshot, and it sends to our API. You go back to work. Email arrives when posts are ready.

Does it auto-post for me?

Not yet (Phase 2). For now, you review posts in the web UI and manually copy-paste to each platform. This keeps you in control and prevents accidents. Auto-scheduling coming later.

What platforms does it support?

Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram, Dev.to, Medium, Hacker News, Reddit, your blog, YouTube Community, and TikTok. HotKey generates format-specific posts for each. You pick which ones to use.

Is my data private?

Yes. We only capture what you explicitly tell us to. The extension doesn't run in the background. Your content is processed to generate posts, then deleted after 30 days. We never train models on your data or share it with anyone.
