# Designed my content factory: 10 steps, 1 daily decision

**Date:** 2026-03-07 10:00
**Pillar:** business-systems
**Score:** 78

## What Happened

Designed a complete content production pipeline for social media: 10 deterministic steps from story capture to published post. Wrote a 1,186-line PRD covering pipeline architecture, data schemas, Publer API integration, a DE-AI rewrite step, a 7-point QA rubric, pre-sell as a first-class content type, and a daily review UI with notification cadence. Scheduling defaults sourced from real research — Buffer analyzed 9.6M Instagram posts, 4.8M LinkedIn posts, and 1M+ tweets.

## Why It Matters

The same factory pattern that works for iOS apps and real estate reels works for content. Most content systems fail at decision-making, not creation — every post requiring a channel, format, and timing call means nothing ships. This pipeline reduces Matt's daily involvement to one decision: approve this week's plan. Everything else — analysis, generation, de-AI pass, QA, scoring, scheduling — is handled by the system.
