# Deleted 44k lines. Rebuilt in 4k. Now it runs a business.

**Date:** 2026-03-07 10:00
**Pillar:** ai
**Score:** 90

## What Happened

Deleted 44,000 lines of over-engineered TypeScript/React from the Business Factory repo and replaced it with 4,000 lines of focused Python. The new system is a deterministic daily loop — lead sourcing, cold email, reply classification, reel fulfillment, and Stripe webhook handling — that runs at 7:30am via launchd. AI is used only at the edges (drafting emails, classifying replies); all decision logic is pure algorithmic code.

## Why It Matters

Most AI agents fail because they're probabilistic end-to-end. The playbook for 0→1 business growth is already known — you don't need AI to figure out the sequence, you need it to execute specific steps well. Separating deterministic orchestration from AI-powered execution makes the system reliable, auditable, and actually shippable.
