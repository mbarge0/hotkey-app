# Market Research to Hi-Fi Screens in One Session

**Date:** 2026-03-06 22:00
**Pillar:** ai
**Score:** 84

## What Happened

Used iosfactory to take a new app idea from zero to hi-fi screen designs in a single session — no designer, no wireframes. AI did competitive market research and identified micro-landlord property management (1-5 units) as a high-opportunity category: ~20M users in the US, competitors with 1.3-star ratings and fraud complaints, existing tools either enterprise-priced or barely functional. Ran full discovery (target user, MVP scope, business model), generated a complete design system with hex codes and typography stack, then fed the spec to Gemini 2.5 Pro which generated HTML/CSS for 5 screens. Playwright rendered each at 390x844 (iPhone 14 Pro) and captured PNGs — HTML deleted immediately, only artifacts kept.

## Why It Matters

The design quality is the real test. Not perfect — status bar icons are basic, gradients a little flat — but the color system, type hierarchy, and component patterns came through production-ready. It demonstrates how much of the research-discovery-design pipeline can be fully automated before human judgment adds real value. The answer right now: further than most people expect.
