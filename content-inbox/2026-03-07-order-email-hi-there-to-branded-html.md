# Order email went from "Hi there" to branded HTML

**Date:** 2026-03-07 14:00
**Pillar:** product
**Score:** 83

## What Happened

The auto-generated order confirmation email was plain text: "Hi there, Got your payment." Rebuilt it as a branded HTML email with Real Estate Reels' navy and gold palette — order summary panel, what's included checklist, and a dark call-to-action block with a single conversational ask instead of any form. Delivery email got the same treatment: production quality notes, 3 ready-to-post captions, posting tips, and a reorder CTA.

## Why It Matters

The product experience doesn't start when the reel lands in their inbox. It starts the moment the confirmation email arrives. Realtors are used to polished marketing — if the order email looks like a side project, the whole product feels like one. The email is the first moment of trust after payment.
